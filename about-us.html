Company Overview


Equipped with more than 20 years of experience working as Engineer abroad and operating its own Industrial Automation Company in Middle East; the owner and partners embarked to a noble idea of bringing the earned knowledge and technical expertise to their localities in Philippines. With the firm belief that life could be enriched by providing modern solutions to the daily needs of public and private sector which could be realized through technology transfer to the rural areas. This collaborative effort bring to life APILTECH SOLUTIONS, an Electrical and Electronics Systems Integration Company that provide wide range of innovative, automated and state of the art solution to the market.

APILTECH SOLUTIONS aims to be the market leader for Electrical Systems, including Power Distribution Board (PDB), Main Distribution Board (MDB), Low Voltage Switch Gear (LVAC) , Motor Control Center (MCC), Uninterrupted Power Supply (UPS), Manual Transfer Switch (MTS), Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS), Solar Power System, Lighting System, Power System, Power Cables and Generators of any size.

APILTECH SOLUTIONS also aims to be the market leader for Security Systems, including CCTV, Burglary and Fire Systems Monitoring, Fire & Safety protection Systems, Paging Systems, Wireless Communications Systems as well as advanced technology products

APILTECH SOLUTIONS as an integrated solutions provider, works with the clients to offer the best solutions that meet their requirements, with very latest technology, high quality, reliability & effective solutions for all Government, Residential, Retail, Commercial, Industrial, & all other markets requiring electrical, automation, security & protection solutions.

APILTECH SOLUTIONS business units, with offices in San Pedro, Irosin, Sorosogon are well placed to cover all aspects of the market, both in Sorsogon and Bicol Region. APILTECH SOLUTIONS offers system design, integration, supervision, installation and customer training, after sales support, all backed up with a fully trained sales & technical staff.

We work closely with our customers, ensuring that the task or project is completed on time and on budget, using the highest quality products available. We also advise our customers of any cost saving measure that can be utilized and can assist with any safety requirements. By using our skills and experience we build ongoing and lasting relationships with our customers.
